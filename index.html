Materials

The Elements of Luxury

Italian Marble

Hand-selected Calacatta and Statuario from Carrara quarries. Each slab tells a story millions of years in the making.

Walnut Wood

American black walnut, kiln-dried and hand-finished with natural oils. Rich grain patterns that deepen with time.

Crystal Glass

Bespoke glasswork with precision-cut edges. From partitions to sculptures, transparency becomes architecture.

Solid Brass

Unlacquered brass that develops a living patina. Hardware, fixtures, and accents that age with character.

Polished Concrete

Architectural concrete with diamond-polished finish. Industrial soul meets refined elegance in every surface.

Natural Stone

Travertine, limestone, and onyx sourced from ancient quarries. Earth textures that ground every space.
